Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Effective date: May 2026

1. Introduction

CercaLabs ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at cercalabs.com or download resources we make available, including white papers and guides.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at info@cercalabs.com.

This policy applies to all information collected through our website and related communications. Please read it carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our site.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you download a resource, fill out a contact form, or otherwise interact with us. The categories of personal information we may collect include:

  • Identifiers: First name, last name, email address
  • Professional information: Job title, organization name, industry
  • Commercial information: Records of resources downloaded and services inquired about
  • Internet or network activity: Pages visited, time on site, referring URL, browser type, and device type (collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools)
  • Geolocation data: General location based on IP address (country/state level only)

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as Social Security numbers, financial account details, health information, biometric data, or precise geolocation.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To deliver requested resources, including white papers, guides, and reports
  • To send you relevant content about RCM automation, healthcare operations, and CercaLabs services — only if you have opted in
  • To respond to inquiries and schedule consultations
  • To analyze how our website and resources are used so we can improve them
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations
  • To protect against fraud, unauthorized activity, and other harmful behavior

We will not use your personal information for any purpose not listed above without first notifying you and, where required by law, obtaining your consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may share information with trusted vendors who assist us in operating our website, sending emails, or analyzing data. These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only to provide services to us and may not use it for their own purposes.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information where required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of CercaLabs, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about how you use our website. This includes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics 4). This data is aggregated and anonymized where possible.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to measure the effectiveness of our content and campaigns

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. We honor browser-based "Do Not Track" signals where technically feasible.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

  • Lead and contact information: retained for up to 3 years from last interaction, unless you request deletion sooner
  • Analytics data: retained per the default settings of our analytics provider (typically 14 months for Google Analytics)
  • Email communication records: retained for up to 2 years

When personal information is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it securely.

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you have reason to believe your interaction with us is no longer secure, please notify us immediately at info@cercalabs.com.

8. Your Rights Under CCPA / CPRA

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), effective January 1, 2026:

Right to Know
Request details about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we've collected about you, how it's used, and with whom it's shared.
Response: within 45 days
Right to Delete
Request deletion of personal information we have collected about you, subject to certain exceptions required by law.
Response: within 45 days
Right to Correct
Request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
Response: within 45 days
Right to Opt Out
Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. We do not sell personal information, but you may submit an opt-out request at any time.
Response: within 15 business days
Right to Limit Use
Limit our use or disclosure of sensitive personal information to only what is necessary to perform the requested service.
Response: within 15 business days
Right to Non-Discrimination
We will never deny services, charge different prices, or provide a lower quality of service because you exercised your privacy rights.
Always honored

To exercise any of these rights, submit a verifiable request to us by emailing info@cercalabs.com or by using our Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information page. We will verify your identity before processing requests to protect your privacy.

You may submit requests on behalf of a minor child. Authorized agents may submit requests on your behalf with written permission.

9. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

CercaLabs does not sell your personal information to third parties. However, under CCPA/CPRA, certain sharing of data with analytics providers may be considered "sharing" under the law. You have the right to opt out of this at any time.

To submit a Do Not Sell or Share request, visit our Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information page or email info@cercalabs.com.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

11. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at info@cercalabs.com.

12. Updates to This Policy

We review and update this Privacy Policy at least once every 12 months as required by CCPA, and whenever our data practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised.

We will notify you of material changes by posting a prominent notice on our website or by sending an email to the address on file, where required by law.
